About me

My name is Yves (Linkedin, GitHub, Bytesupply) and I live in Florida, USA. I graduated the Royal Athenaeum with a degree in Humanities & Science in Ghent, Belgium.

In the mid 80's, after fulfilling my military duty (Lieutenant - 10th Mech Brigade - 2nd Artillery Regiment - Bravo battery - NATO, Germany), I immigrated to the US.

I have been working as a software engineer for more than 25 years, mostly in Los Angeles and New York City.

Additionally I spent about 10 years in aviation. In the airline industry as a FAR Part 121 Flight Dispatcher/Operational Controller and owned a small flightschool specializing in pilot instrument certification.

Technically, I enjoy Golang and Python but I've been programming in, and using technologies such as PostgreSQL, mongoDB, MySQL, AMQP, REST API, gRPC and much more.

In the past I worked with C, Java, PHP, Assembly (WD16, M680x0) and some others I am no longer proficient in and some that are no longer relevant today.

As a hobby, I enjoy working with microcontrollers in real world settings.

I have a track record of building systems  which have stood the test of time - many of which are still running today after many years.
